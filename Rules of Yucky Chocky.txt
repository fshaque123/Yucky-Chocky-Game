Rules of Yucky Chocky:Yucky Chocky is a simple game, and easy to represent in a graphical user interface.Two players begin with a rectangular bar of chocolate, itself composed of smaller squares.Each turn a player must break off a lump of chocolate. The break must be a single straight line cutting all the way across the rectangle along the lines between the squares.However the top-left square has been replaced with a piece of soap. The loser is the person who has to eat the soap.
